Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order. Can't find your answer? Just ask us on WhatsApp!

Do you deliver? How far?+

Yes! We deliver within Nairobi. Delivery charges depend on your location and will be confirmed when you place your order. Message us on WhatsApp with your estate/area and we'll confirm availability and cost.

How far in advance should I order?+

We bake on Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays. You should place your order the day before your chosen baking day. For large or custom orders (events, birthdays), we recommend giving us at least 3–5 days notice so we can plan ahead.

What are the minimum order quantities?+

Minimum orders are: Cake Pops (6 pcs), Cakesicles (6 pcs), Artisan Cookies (6 pcs), Cupcakes (6 pcs), Muffins (6 pcs), Cake Slices (4 pcs), Banana Bread (1 loaf). These minimums ensure freshness and quality for every order.

Can I choose my own flavours?+

Absolutely! We offer Vanilla, Orange, Cookies & Cream, Red Velvet, Biscoff, and Caramel — and we're open to other flavour requests too. Just let us know when ordering and we'll do our best to accommodate. Available flavours may vary week to week.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept M-Pesa and cash on delivery. Payment details will be confirmed via WhatsApp when your order is ready. We typically require payment before or on delivery.

Do you do custom or bulk orders for events?+

Yes! We love doing custom orders for birthdays, baby showers, corporate events, weddings, and more. We can customise colours, packaging, and flavours to match your theme. Reach out on WhatsApp with your event details and we'll create something special.

Are there any allergens I should know about?+

All our treats contain gluten (wheat flour), dairy (butter, cream, milk), and eggs. Some products may contain nuts. Our kitchen is home-based and NOT allergen-free — cross-contamination is possible. Always mention any allergies when ordering so we can advise appropriately.

How should I store my treats?+

🍭 Cake Pops & Cakesicles: cool dry place or fridge, best within 5–7 days. 🍪 Cookies: airtight container at room temperature, best within 1 week. 🍌 Banana Bread: wrap well, room temp 3–4 days or fridge up to 1 week. 🧁 Cupcakes & Muffins: airtight container, best within 3–4 days. 🎂 Cake Slices: refrigerate, enjoy within 3–4 days.

Can I pick up instead of getting delivery?+

Yes! Collection is available and saves you the delivery charge. Once your order is confirmed, we'll share our location and arrange a convenient pickup time. Message us on WhatsApp to arrange this.

How do I place an order?+

The easiest way is to use our "Build a Package" tool on this website, then tap "Send Order on WhatsApp" — your full order drops straight into a chat with us. Or you can go to "Order Now" and message us directly. We usually reply within a few hours!
